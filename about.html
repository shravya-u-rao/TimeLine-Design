Compassionate and Experienced Team

Our team of compassionate professionals, including therapists, educators, and support of staff, is passionate about making a positive difference in the lives of individuals with autism. With years of experience and expertise, we are committed to providing the highest quality care and support.

Individualized Programs

We believe in the power of personalized care. Each individual at Oranges Care receives an individualized program tailored to their unique strengths, challenges, and goals. We work closely with families to develop strategies that promote growth, development, and independence.

Holistic Approach

We take a holistic approach to autism care, focusing not only on academic and behavioral development but also on social skills, communication, sensory integration, and overall well-being. Our comprehensive approach ensures that individuals receive the support they need in all areas of their lives.

State-of-the-Art Facilities

Oranges Care is equipped with state-of-the-art facilities and resources designed to create a supportive and engaging environment. From sensory rooms to therapeutic equipment, we provide the tools necessary for individuals to learn, grow, and thrive.
